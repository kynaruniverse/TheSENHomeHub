Privacy Policy

We collect almost nothing. Here's exactly what we do and don't do.

Last updated: January 2026

The short version

The SEN Home Hub does not collect personal data, does not use tracking cookies, does not serve ads, and does not require you to create an account. Your saved resources are stored only on your own device using localStorage — we never see them.

Who we are

The SEN Home Hub is a free, independent website for UK families home educating children with special educational needs. It is run by a small team and is not affiliated with any government body, school, or commercial organisation.

What data we collect

We do not collect personal data through normal use of the website. Specifically:

  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics cookies
  • We do not use Google Analytics or any third-party analytics service
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not require you to log in or create an account
  • Your "saved resources" are stored entirely in your browser's localStorage — this data never leaves your device and we cannot access it

If you contact us

If you use the contact form, the information you provide (name, email if given, and message) is used only to respond to your enquiry. We do not share this with any third parties and we do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Third-party services

We use Google Fonts to display our chosen typefaces. Google's own privacy policy applies to this service. No other third-party services are used on this website.

Children's privacy

The Children's Zone of this website is designed for use by children, but no personal data about children is collected at any point. The interactive tools (phonics board, maths board, etc.) run entirely in the browser and do not send any data to any server.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. Since we hold essentially no personal data (unless you have contacted us), this is unlikely to be relevant — but if you have concerns, please get in touch.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page if our practices change. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
